One way cows help the environment is by reducing the risk of range fires. When grass get unreasonably tall and dry, it is really easy for a stray match, lightning or even sparks from cars and ATVs to start a range fire. These fires can burn thousands of acres, ruin habitat for deer, birds and other animals, and sometimes threaten the homes of families.Cows provide us with important byproducts.Cattle eat the grass that is grown by the sun to power themselves. They use the energy to make muscle which is what makes up beef!
